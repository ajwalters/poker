        <description>Ran into this while working on a Deal Bank bug. A little history:

Deal Bank deals have a custom user-specific 2-day counter, so they replace the countdown clock on the personalize.js call. On the deal page, this was working fine, but on the featured deal page, it would rapidly flicker back and forth (every second) between the correct 2-day countdown and some other countdown.

We discovered that the featured deal page pulls the time to count down to from a deal_deadline variable set by attribute_for in the html. This means that the deadline for the default deal for a division is hardcoded into the page and cached in the CDN.

The implication: On the personalize.js call, if the default division deal is replaced by a more relevant deal for the user, the countdown timer may still be counting down to the _old_ deal.

This story is an investigation into whether this is actually happening. See Jeff L with questions.</description>
